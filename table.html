School of Engineering Frontend Engineering It is designed to provide you with in-depth knowledge on how to create functional websites through the use of various programming languages.

Cybersecurity Become well-equipped to enter the workforce with a solid foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of cybersecurity, with no degree or prior experience required
